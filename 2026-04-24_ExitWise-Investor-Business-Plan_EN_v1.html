03

Problem

Exit-stage real-estate transactions are still executed the way they were in the 1990s — manually.

IM production: 2–4 weeks · USD 4,000–15,000

At Korean brokerage firms, a single IM still consumes weeks of analyst time at significant labor cost.

10+ data sources assembled by hand

Appraisals, public-data APIs, DART, registry, central-bank data, mapping — all stitched together manually.

NPL and workout expertise is scarce

Complex deals — especially in the mid-market — go untouched. Fertile ground for AI standardization.

Cross-border translation and reformat burden

English, Chinese, and Japanese IMs require added cost and rework. No integrated domestic solution exists.

EFFECT With AI, this collapses to 2–4 hours per IM and a per-unit cost below 1/50 of manual production.
